# Prompt Template for ChatGPT (TRP Drafting)

Use only the attached files as source material.

## Task

Draft a complete **Thesis Research Plan (TRP)** in English for this OT cybersecurity project.

## Hard constraints

1. Do not invent empirical results or deployment outcomes.
2. Distinguish clearly between:
   - already validated facts (checkpoint evidence), and
   - proposed future research work.
3. Follow this required scope:
   - context, motivation, objectives,
   - early thesis statement,
   - state of the art and research gap,
   - preliminary work,
   - commented workplan with Gantt.
4. Use active voice and first-person where appropriate.
5. Keep technical terminology consistent (Modbus/TCP, CT1..CT7, interface attribution, distributed sensors, SPAN/TAP).

## Required TRP structure

1. Title
2. Context and Motivation
3. Research Problem and Objectives
4. Early Thesis Statement
5. State of the Art and Gap Analysis
6. Preliminary Work and Current Evidence
7. Proposed Research Methodology
8. Workplan and Gantt
9. Risks, Limitations, and Mitigation
10. Expected Contributions
11. References section placeholder

## Writing behavior

- Be explicit when making assumptions.
- Keep claims traceable to the evidence files.
- Add concise transition text between sections to preserve narrative coherence.

## Output requested

1. Full TRP draft text.
2. Evidence-traceability appendix mapping major factual claims to source files.
3. A short list of supervisor-review questions to validate scope and feasibility.
